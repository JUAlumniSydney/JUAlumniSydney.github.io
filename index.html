Welcome to Jadavpur University Alumni Sydney

Jadavpur University Alumni Association Sydney ( Australia) Chapter was formed in 1995 to provide a suitable social and intellectual platform for all ex students to keep the JU flag flying in their minds ; to reminiscence the good time at the pioneering institution and share moments of joy and pride with others.

In line with objectives of our parent body our key focus is to:

  • To foster a spirit of comradeship and to promote co-operation and mutual help amongst the
    ex-students of
  • To foster the growth of healthy fellow-feelings, to promote common interest and to maintain a feeling of unity amongst the ex-students.
  • To organize and develop the activities of the ex-students on matters such as social, economic, educational and philanthropic.
