Fellowship

Recently Saha has been elected as a fellow of Engineers Australia for his high standard professional achievements and contribution to the profession. He was a manager of the SMaRT Centre at UNSW. With his excellent contribution to the centre and their PhD research students the centre has grown to be a leading national and international force within the materials science and recycling technologies sector. He is co-author of twenty-one papers published in high grade international journals and twenty conference papers. He retired from UNSW in 2015 and is now a visiting academic at the engineering faculty at UNSW.
